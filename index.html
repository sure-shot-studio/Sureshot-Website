SURE SHOT.

Ren F is a Miami-based photographer and founder of Sure Shot Studio LLC. Shooting since 2013 with Sony and Canon, Ren documents the intersection of hip-hop culture, live performance, street life, and toy collecting — from front-row concert pits to intimate studio setups.

Whether it’s a Wu-Tang show, a vinyl toy on a black background, or a Tron arcade cabinet glowing in the dark — every frame is a Sure Shot.
